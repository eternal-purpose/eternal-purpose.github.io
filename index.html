We are Eternal Purpose

David Tatler – Songwriter, rhythm guitar

From Stourbridge, West Midlands. Inspired by The Beatles, he learned the guitar and formed his first band whilst still at school and played in several bands until his mid twenties.


Fast‐forward to 2014 and having been diagnosed with cancer, he rediscovered his love of music and began writing and recording songs in his home studio.

Tim Matthews – Vocalist, songwriter, keyboards, lead and bass guitar

Originally from Croydon in London. Part of several bands in his early years, one of which became professional in America. Tim, however, stayed in the UK and set up a recording studio in London. He made several albums with various bands.


When he moved to the Midlands he converted his garage into a recording facility where the initial Eternal Purpose demos were made.

Barbie Irwine – Vocalist and songwriter

Originally from the West Midlands. Worked in the holiday entertainment industry during the 1990s.


She has since performed around the UK and in Europe and has recorded as one half of a duo and also as a solo artist.
